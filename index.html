About The Game

Tibetan Quest: Beyond the World's End is a mesmerizing hidden object game that masterfully combines thriller and adventure elements with oriental mysticism and ancient lore. When your beloved niece disappears during her archaeological expedition to Tibet, you must embark on a dangerous journey to find her.
